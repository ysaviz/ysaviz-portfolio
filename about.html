Currently Studying at SJSU, Digital Media Arts

My name is Ysa Vizconde and Art means numerous things to me. I generally have a wide range of different types of art. I have made cochlear implant pieces, art of my pets, and people. I take inspiration from cartoon shows growing up like, Adventure Time, or just general artists I follow on Instagram. Through my art, I create fun and interesting to me and I include a lot of shapes and little doodles to contrast the negative space. I produce whatever I believe is eye catching and intriguing, hopefully reaching the right . I look forward to doodling various components whenever I can. I am looking forward to learn and create a game of some sort in the future. I give my thanks to Night In The Woods for giving me a deep feeling for storytelling while having a beautiful scenery, I want to be able to construct a game like that. I believe that in order to make art of some sort, I will truly feel successful in it if I can make someone feel a strong emotion towards it. I wish my art can reach people's hearts with personal pieces, like a blissful memory.
